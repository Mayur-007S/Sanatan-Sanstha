## 1. Color Palette

### Primary Colors (Spiritual & Warmth)
- **Saffron/Orange** (Represents Energy & Spirituality)
  - Text: `text-orange-800` (#9a3412)
  - Border: `border-orange-100` (#ffedd5)
  - Backgrounds: `bg-orange-50` (#fff7ed)

### Secondary Colors (Calm & Trust)
- **Blue** (Represents Clarity & Stability)
  - Text: `text-blue-800` (#1e40af)
  - Border: `border-blue-100` (#dbeafe)
  - Backgrounds: `bg-blue-50` (#eff6ff)

### Neutral Colors (readability)
- **Slate** (Modern & Clean)
  - Primary Text: `text-slate-900` (#0f172a)
  - Secondary Text: `text-slate-600` (#475569)
  - Borders: `border-slate-200` (#e2e8f0)
  - Backgrounds: `bg-slate-50` (#f8fafc)

## 2. Typography

### Font Family
- **Primary:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` (Google Fonts)
  - Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold)

### Hierarchy
- **App Title:** `text-2xl font-bold uppercase tracking-wide`
- **Section Headers:** `text-lg font-bold`
- **Body Text:** `text-sm font-medium`
- **Captions/Labels:** `text-[10px] uppercase font-bold tracking-wider`

## 3. UI Components

### Buttons
- **Primary Action:** `bg-emerald-600 text-white hover:bg-emerald-700`
- **Secondary Action:** `bg-white border hover:bg-slate-50`
- **Icon Buttons:** `p-2 rounded-full hover:bg-orange-200/50`

### Cards
- `bg-white rounded-3xl shadow-xl border border-slate-100`

### Input Fields
- `rounded-xl border-slate-200 bg-slate-50 focus:ring-emerald-500`

## 4. Accessibility
- Ensure all text has sufficient contrast.
- Use `aria-label` for icon-only buttons.
- Touch targets must be at least 44x44px (or include padding).
